## Digital Sovereignty and Localization of Aid

Developed for the National Council of Humanitarian and Development NGO Fora (CONAFOHD), KOLData is a platform designed to centralize and track NGO activities in the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

The core challenge is to address the lack of visibility regarding actions led by national NGOs. Too often, humanitarian data is collected by international actors without benefiting local structures.
This platform enables the centralization and promotion of data produced by over 500 Congolese organizations in order to:

- **Strengthen advocacy** and the recognition of local initiatives among donors.
- **Improve coordination** and planning of humanitarian and development interventions across the entire territory.
- **Provide a secure tool** for data editing by member organizations within the five regional fora.

## Technical Implementation

- **Tech Stack**: User interface developed with Vue.js and Maplibre for the mapping component. Supabase for the database and authentication.
- **RLS Policies**: Implementation of Row Level Security (RLS) policies to secure data and ensure that each organization can only access its own data.
- **Rapid Deployment**: As a pilot project with a limited budget, choosing a modern stack allowed for the deployment of a full platform in just a few days.
